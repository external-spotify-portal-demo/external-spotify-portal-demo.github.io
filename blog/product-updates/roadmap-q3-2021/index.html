Product roadmap: Q3 2021

July 13, 2023 · One min read
Francesco Corti, Spotify

It’s here! The latest and greatest roadmap for Backstage includes plans for:

  • Performance and stability improvements to the Backstage core
  • More elegant failure and recovery modes for Software Templates
  • The ability to remove duplicates from the Software Catalog
  • Next steps for getting TechDocs to beta
  • Integrating Elasticsearch to the new Search platform
  • A whole lot more

Have feedback on the product roadmap or want to contribute? We want to hear from you!

Read on to learn how to influence the roadmap and help shape the future of developer experience with us. As you can see, there’s a lot of good work ahead. Let’s get started.
